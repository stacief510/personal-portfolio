Hi, I'm Stacie

Full-Stack Software Engineer

Growing up in the San Francisco Bay Area, I was fortunate to be immersed in the possibilities of technology and watch it shape our world and future. Although technology has always been something I was interested in, I decided to go to college for a degree in English Literature, as I also love to read.

After college, I spent some years working in the corporate world trying to decide how to best live my life. It wasn’t until 2017 that I decided to take a part-time Front End Web Development course at General Assembly. I learned HTML, CSS, JQuery, and Javascript. Although learning these languages were enough to become a web designer, it didn’t quench my desire of understanding the Internet from all angles. It was there that I realized my passion to create and learn, and thus my journey into web development begun.

I am currently enrolled in General Assembly’s full-time web development immersive course. Here, I am gaining an even deeper understanding of Javascript and will also learn other languages like React and Ruby on Rails. By the end of this course, I will be ready to shape our world and future.
